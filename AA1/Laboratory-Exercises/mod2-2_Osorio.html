To The Abandoned Sacred Beasts

To The Abandoned Sacred Beasts is a Japanese dark fantasy manga series featuring a "beast hunter", Hank Henriette, Captain of the Incarnates Soldier Unit and known as the werewolf Incarnate. When the war ended, Hank began travelling the country to kill his former allies who have lost their souls and broke their promise made with him during the Civil War
