Traditional Mediums

Besides digital art, I also do traditional...well, everyone always starts out with a piece of paper and a pencil, but if I want to do something quickly with less the effort, I always go for the Iconic tradiitonal paper and pencil. I use this medium whenever i plan out designs for characters i will be using in my art pieces like this one, this is a reference sheet for one of my characters named Michaela. I also use paints and other materials for more detailed artowkrs but the tools I always have with me are pens, pencils, and paper.
