Soft colors test

This artpiece meanwhile is one of my experiments when it comes to monotone-like colors, for this I used the color pink because it looked soft. Back then I usually went for more dark toned or highly saturated colors when it comes to coloring but this time I thought "what if i chose softer colors" and so I began to implement more softer colors for some art pieces if I ever need it for more aesthetic uses, they were more calming to the eyes but getting the right color is sometimes difficult.

Description:

This website would also showcase what styles i can do and what i tend to avoid drawing due to personal preferences.
