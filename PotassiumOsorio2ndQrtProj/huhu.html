Stylized Portraits

Since a few months ago, I have been practicing making drawings of my friends to get a better grasp of human proportions, poses, and overall how to draw more accurate human faces since realism hasn't been my cup of tea. As an artist, I strive to enhance my capabilities and expand my portfolio, thus, adding portraits to my list of "Things I can draw decently" just incase i wanted to earn some cash on the side.
