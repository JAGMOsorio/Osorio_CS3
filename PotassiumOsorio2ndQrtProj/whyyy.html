Arts club

With countless of schoolworks piled up, it was a good decision that i joined a club that had my interest, SINAG has really helped me push through my limits with the given prompts and improved my confidence as well for the weekly art prompt presentations. For the 3rd week of November, I've made an artpiece for dias de los muertos (day of the dead), one of my most tedious pieces yet, consisting of many many layers but in the end it was worth it to see the reactions of my peers, which i can say were all positive!
