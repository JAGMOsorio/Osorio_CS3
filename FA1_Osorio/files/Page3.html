Dark Chocolate

  • Dark chocolate is packed full of important minerals, including iron, magnesium, zinc, copper and phosphorus
  • Dark chocolate has more cacao and less sugar than other chocolates, so it is considered healthier than milk and white chocolate..
  • I like dark chocolate because of it's interesting flavor
  • Dark chocolate can be considered Vegan, allowed in a keto diet, and halal
